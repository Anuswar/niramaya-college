About Us

Home → About Us

The success of our students and graduates is the prime focus of Niramaya College of Nursing & Allied sciences. Nurses are much important to the healthcare sector for proper development and sustainment. Our faculties and staff are dedicated to equipping learners with the necessary knowledge and skills to greatly improve the care outcomes and the quality of life they serve.

Our Values

Vision

To be a leading institution fostering innovation and excellence in education.

Mission

To empower students with skills and knowledge to succeed in a dynamic world.

Core Values

We have an excellent infrastructure with conducive learning environment like library facilities, internet and also hostel facilities with all the available amenities to support the student study and pleasant stay here.
