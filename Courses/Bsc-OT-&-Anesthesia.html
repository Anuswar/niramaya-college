BSc Anesthesia & Operation Theatre Technology

Duration: 4 years

The course involves the study of anesthesia techniques, equipment, and dosage required for medical and surgical operations, along with operation theatre management and maintenance.

Students are trained in patient monitoring, sterilization, and teamwork skills necessary for precision and hygiene in healthcare settings.

Scope after Course

  • Teacher/Lecturer
  • Research Associate
  • Anesthesia Technician
  • Medical Case Review Physician
  • Patient Care Coordinator
  • Medical Consultant
  • ECMO Technician
  • Associate Consultant
  • OT Technician
  • OT Assistant
  • Lab Technician

Eligibility

  • Passed higher secondary (10+2) or equivalent with at least 50% marks.

  • Maximum age limit: 35 years.
