Bsc Nursing

Duration: 4 years

Nursing is a challenging service provided to human beings who are unable to meet their basic needs due to illness or challenging medical conditions. Nursing is a career, rich in opportunities. This is well known that there has always been a great demand globally for good quality service nurses.

The changes in the human lifestyle, changing disease patterns, technological development and scientific advancement in diagnostic measures and treatment modalities have modified the role of nurses to the next and more complex level. BSC Nursing is the minimum qualification recognised by the global health departments for practising nursing as a profession globally

Looking into the changing scenario RK College and School of nursing is keen to provide the best and latest knowledge to our students for pursuing their careers from any part of the world. Our graduates hold significant positions in the hospital community and Nursing Education. Opportunities for further studies in the field are also high for aspiring students

Scope after Course

Nurse educator, Nurse practitioner, Nurse advocate, Nurse Midwife, Mental Health Nurse, Nurse counsellor, Community Health Nurse, Nurse Specialist, Nurse Administer, Care Giver, Nurse Researcher, etc.

Eligibility

  • The candidate should have passed higher secondary (10+2) or equivalent with 50% total marks
  • Must have studied Physics, Chemistry, Biology studied together and passed in English individually in class 12th.
  • The minimum age for admission shall be 17 years on 31st December of the year of admission and the maximum age limit shall be 35 years.
  • Proof of Identity and Birth as recognised by the government, along with 3 passport-size photos needs to be presented at the time of admission.
